# EHT image licences

The Event Horizon Telescope (EHT) Collaboration releases its imagery under
Creative Commons licences. Each file below is attributed here and repeated in
the product UI that renders it, per the licence terms.

## m87.jpg

- **Subject:** M87\* — supermassive black hole at the centre of the elliptical
  galaxy Messier 87, first direct image of a black hole.
- **Release:** 10 April 2019.
- **Credit:** Event Horizon Telescope Collaboration.
- **Source:** ESO mirror, `eso1907a` (<https://www.eso.org/public/images/eso1907a/>).
- **Licence:** Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0).
- **Attribution string used in-app:** "M87\* — Event Horizon Telescope
  Collaboration, 10 April 2019."

## sgr-a.jpg

- **Subject:** Sagittarius A\* (Sgr A\*) — supermassive black hole at the

- **Release:** 12 May 2022.
- **Credit:** Event Horizon Telescope Collaboration.
- **Source:** ESO mirror, `eso2208-eht-mwa` (<https://www.eso.org/public/images/eso2208-eht-mwa/>).
